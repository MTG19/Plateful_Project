Burger Sandwich

Crumble the bread into small pieces. Sprinkle with water, cover with a damp cloth and leave for 30 minutes. Heat 2-3 tbsp of oil in a deep pan. Add the garlic cloves, chopped onion, and your favorite spices. Add the bread and stir continuously for 5 minutes. Set the pan aside. In a separate frying pan, sauté the meat or any desired ingredients. Combine everything, stirring constantly for 15 minutes and add a grinding of black pepper. Add some broth or water if needed. Cook for about 20 minutes. It will be ready when the bread is soft and golden.

Ingredients

  • Olive Oil: 2-3 tbsp
  • Garlic: 2 cloves
  • Bread: 1 loaf
  • Onion: 1 small
  • Pork: Optional
